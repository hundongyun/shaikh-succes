The true story behind Gazal Shaikh’s success

Home / Uncategorized / The true story behind Gazal Shaikh’s success
Twila Strothers   Uncategorized   0   November 22, 2019

Gazal Shaikh is a fashion and lifestyle blogger headquartered in Qatar, showcasing all the best events in Qatar. She’s famous with quality content and honest feedback. She is also an extremely talented fashion designer based in Qatar. IAMGAZAL was born in 2014 as a result of her creative aspirations.

Gazal’s primary focus is not only on encouraging fashion and lifestyle through her blog, but she also wants people to be better versions of themselves, and that’s why she shares her skills and knowledge on a timely basis. One should not rely on mere materialistic needs, but family and self-growth are important. IAMGAZAL is increasing its popularity over time not only because of its valuable content and reliable reviews but also because of its frank and forward-looking claims. She claims that human beings must be treated fairly wherever they come from.

Via her blog, IAMGAZAL, she continues to innovate and encourage others by voicing her ideas and opinions to make them feel better about themselves. Efforts to encourage, educate and empowering women around the globe through style tips, fashion trends, personal style, product reviews, lifestyle, beauty secrets.  That led her to become a collaborator with brands such as Rolls Royce, Victoria Secret, Massimo Dutti, Versace, Master Card, Hyundai, Swissline Skincare, The Pearl Qatar (UDC), West Elm, to name a few.

She is devoted to quality over quantity, which is why Gazal is very cautious when dealing with any brand and does her homework before endorsing anything on her blog by checking the performance and credibility of the product and business.

That article she produces is an imaginative musical, designed for the public as well as classes that offer the best and the newest in fashion through collaborations with the best in the industry. Fulfilling her creative drive by producing quality creations and sharing those with love with her viewers around the world.
